Build a Plan

2/3 days a week

Complete the following routine two to three time a week leaving at least one day between workouts.

Select whichever compound or isolation exercises you choose to complete the plan. These exercises can be found for each muscle on the other pages of this website.

MUSCLE EXERCISE SETS REPS REST
Chest Compound 1 12 1min
Back Compound 1 12 1min
Shoulders Compound 1 12 1min
Legs Compound 1 12 1min
Abs Abdominals 1 20 1min

After completeing every exercise take a 2min rest. Complete the circuit 3 times in total.

4 days a week

This plan is split into 2 workout that are each completed twice a week.

Select whichever compound or isolation exercises you choose to complete the plan. These exercises can be found for each muscle on the other pages of this website.

Days 1 & 3
MUSCLE EXERCISE SETS REPS REST
Chest Compound 4 8 1.5mins
Chest Isolation 3 12 1min
Back Compound 4 8 1.5mins
Back Isolation 3 12 1min
Abs Abdominals 3 20 30secs
Days 2 & 4
MUSCLE EXERCISE SETS REPS REST
Legs Compound 4 10 1.5mins
Legs Isolation 3 15 1min
Shoulders Compound 4 8 1.5mins
Shoulders Isolation 3 12 1min
Abs Obliques 4 20 30secs

Complete every set for each exercise before moving onto the next exercise.
